Rhacophorus kio

Black-Webbed Flying Frog



Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Chordata
Class: Amphibia
Order: Anura
Family: Rhacophoridae
Genus: Rhacophorus

These frogs possess large webbed feet and skin flaps on their sides, which act like parachutes, allowing them to make controlled glides from the treetops to the forest floor or to neighboring trees.
